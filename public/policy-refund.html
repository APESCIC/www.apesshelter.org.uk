Refund Policy

Clearer expectations around refunds and admin-fee reviews.

The governance review identifies this public refund policy as effective from 19 June 2025, with a general non-refundable rule and a narrow admin-fee review route.

1

Broad scope

The public policy applies across donations, reservations, adoptions, memberships and other payments.

2

Default position

The normal rule described in the review is that payments are non-refundable.

3

Admin-fee review

A limited route exists to request review of an identified admin fee if a written request is made within 14 days and APES approves it.

4

Legal framing

The policy also includes a governing-law position and contact route for refund-related questions.

What to keep in mind

  • Do not assume donations or support payments can be reversed on request.
  • If you believe an identified admin fee should be reviewed, act quickly and in writing.
  • Keep receipts, dates and clear reasoning if you raise a refund query.
  • Read any route-specific terms too, because service paperwork can add extra detail.

Support route

If you have a payment question, use the contact page or open a ticket so it can be routed correctly with the right details attached.
