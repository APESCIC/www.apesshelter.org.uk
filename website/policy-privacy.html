Privacy Policy

How information is used, shared and protected.

The attached governance review describes the privacy page as the site's effective data-protection notice, covering collection, use, sharing, security and GDPR rights.

Information collected

The policy covers personal details and interaction data collected through websites or services.

Why it is used

Data may be used to deliver services, manage communications, process requests and operate systems safely.

Who it is shared with

Your rights

GDPR-related rights such as access, correction and other privacy requests are part of the public notice.

What visitors should know

  • Cookies and third-party links can involve external providers or technologies.
  • Data security is addressed, but visitors should still use normal account and privacy caution online.
  • Privacy wording can change over time, so revised notices should be reviewed when updated.
  • The governance review noted the legacy page did not visibly display a revision date at the top.

Common reasons to use this page

  • Understanding what happens to enquiry data
  • Checking whether external service providers are involved
  • Finding the right route for privacy or GDPR-related questions
  • Reading about cookies and third-party links before using web tools
